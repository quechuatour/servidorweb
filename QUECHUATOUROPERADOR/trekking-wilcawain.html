Trekking Wilcahuain - Monterrey
Duration: 01 Day
Duration: 05 hrs. Aprox.
Difficulty: Easy

09:00 AM Departure from the city of Huaraz in private transportation to the archaeological remains of Wilcahuain where we will be visiting the archaeological remains, small and large, the place lends itself to appreciate the snow-capped Vallunaraju, Rima rima and other snow-capped mountains, from this place we will begin the 2 hours walk approx. to get to the thermal baths of Monterrey, time for your visit or take a bath in the natural waters of a temperature of 49º, then board the return transport to the city of Huaraz.

Our Service Includes:

- Traslado: Huaraz – Wilcahuain / Monterrey – Huaraz.
- Guía especializado en trekking.
