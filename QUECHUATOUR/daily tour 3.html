Laguna 69

ITINERARY

Duration: 01 Days

FULL DAY: Laguna de Llanganuco / Callejón de Huaylas

We leave the city very early towards the northern part, arriving at the City of Yungay, 55 km from Huaraz. From here we enter the eastern part entering the Quebrada de Llanganuco approx. 28 km. Arriving at the base camp of Cebollapampa (3900 m.) From here we will start the trek of 3 hours approx. To get to Laguna 69 (4,400 m.) From an impressive panorama from here we will see the Nevados: Huascarán, Chopicalqui, Pisco, Huandoy, Yanapaccha and Chacraraju. After staying good time in the lagoon we will return to Cebollapampa and in the return we will see the lagoons of Llanganuco (Chinancocha, Horconcocha), Yungay - Huaraz
